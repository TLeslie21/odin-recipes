Sloppy Joe

Description

Are you tired and looking for a fast and tasty dish to feed the family for the night? Well look no further. This recipe takes no longer than 15 minutes and can easily feed the family for a few days or more.

Ingredients

  • Medium sized non-stick pan
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • Sloppy joe sauce
  • Hamburger buns
  • Green peppers (optional)
  • Seasoning of your choice

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in the pan
  2. Once fully browned and cooked fully, add the sloppy joe sauce (and peppers and seasoning) into the pan
  3. Stir while on low-medium heat for a few minutes
  4. Serve on hamburger buns
  5. Enjoy!
